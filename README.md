## Investment Thesis

Dixon Technologies is India's largest Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) provider — the structural beneficiary of India's PLI scheme and the global China+1 supply chain shift. The company manufactures smartphones, LED TVs, washing machines, and lighting products on contract for Samsung, Motorola, Oppo, Xiaomi, and Signify.

**The tension in this thesis:** DCF (₹7,500) and comps (₹13,200+) point in opposite directions. The DCF reflects conservative execution assumptions — no Vivo JV, modest margin expansion. The comps reflect the sector premium that India's EMS story commands. The stock currently sits between the two, pricing in *partial* execution of management's ₹1,00,000 Cr revenue target. HOLD until one side wins.
